An archive of Midi's

Midi is a universal way of creating and listening to notes, which make music. You have to see midi as a digital pianosheet for your computer!
On this page I've archived some cool midi songs that I think you'll like.

Wondering if you can hotlink these midi's onto your own site? Ofcourse you can! Please leave a message in my guestbook if you do so, with a link to your website. I'll give you a visit!
